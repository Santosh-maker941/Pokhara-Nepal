About Pokhara

"फेवातालको शान्त किनारामा, माछापुच्छ्रेको अनुपम छायाँ"

Pokhara, known as the "City of Lakes," is a mesmerizing blend of nature and culture. Nestled at an elevation of 822 meters, it serves as the gateway to the world-famous Annapurna Circuit. With the stunning reflections of the sacred Machhapuchhre (Fishtail) mountain in the calm waters of Phewa Lake and a vibrant lakeside atmosphere, Pokhara is truly a traveler's paradise.

Whether you are seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures like paragliding and trekking, or peaceful moments at the World Peace Pagoda, Pokhara offers an unforgettable experience for every visitor.

Tourist Spots In Pokhara

Fewa Lake

Fewa (Phewa) Lake in Pokhara, Nepal, is the second-largest lake in the country, famous for its serene waters reflecting the Annapurna mountains, the iconic island Tal Barahi Temple, and bustling Lakeside area with cafes and shops, making it a prime spot for boating, sunset views, and soaking in Pokhara's stunning scenery.

"पोखरा, नेपालमा रहेको फेवा ताल देशको दोस्रो ठूलो ताल हो। यो ताल अन्नपूर्ण हिमालको प्रतिबिम्ब देखिने शान्त पानी, प्रख्यात ताल बाराही मन्दिर, र क्याफे तथा पसलहरूले भरिएको bustling लेकसाइड क्षेत्रका लागि प्रसिद्ध छ। यसले गर्दा यो ताल डुङ्गा सयर, सूर्यास्तको दृश्य हेर्न र पोखराको मनमोहक दृश्यमा रमाउनका लागि एक उत्तम स्थान बनेको छ।"

History Culture

Sarangkot

Sarangkot is a famous hilltop village near Pokhara, Nepal, renowned for its stunning panoramic sunrise/sunset views of the Annapurna range (Dhaulagiri, Machhapuchhre, etc.), Pokhara Valley, and Phewa Lake, offering adventure activities like paragliding, ziplining, and hiking, accessible by taxi or hike for incredible Himalayan vistas and a mix of local culture and nature.

"सरङकोट पोखरा नजिकैको एउटा प्रसिद्ध डाँडाको गाउँ हो। यो स्थान अन्नपूर्ण हिमशृङ्खला (धौलागिरि, माछापुछ्रे, आदि), पोखरा उपत्यका र फेवा तालको मनमोहक सूर्योदय/सूर्यास्त दृश्यका लागि प्रख्यात छ। साथै, प्याराग्लाइडिङ, जिपलाइनिङ र पदयात्रा (हाइकिङ) जस्ता साहसिक गतिविधिहरूका लागि पनि यो लोकप्रिय छ। ट्याक्सी चढेर वा पदयात्रा गरेर पुग्न सकिने यो ठाउँ अविश्वसनीय हिमालय दृश्यहरू र स्थानीय संस्कृति र प्रकृतिको अनुभवका लागि उत्तम गन्तव्य हो।"

World Peace Pagoda

"World Peace Pagoda" (or Shanti Stupa) refers to a series of Buddhist monuments built worldwide to promote non-violence and universal peace. Most were inspired by Nichidatsu Fujii (1885–1985), a Japanese Buddhist monk who founded the Nipponzan-Myōhōji order after meeting Mahatma Gandhi

"विश्व शान्ति स्तूप (World Peace Pagoda) भन्नाले अहिंसा र विश्वव्यापी शान्तिलाई प्रवर्द्धन गर्न विश्वभर निर्माण गरिएका बौद्ध स्मारकहरूको श्रृंखलालाई बुझाउँछ। यीमध्ये अधिकांश स्तूपहरू जापानी बौद्ध भिक्षु निचिदात्सु फुजी (१८८५–१९८५) बाट प्रेरित छन्, जसले महात्मा गान्धीसँगको भेटपछि 'निप्पोनजान-म्योहोजी' (Nipponzan-Myōhōji) सम्प्रदायको स्थापना गरेका थिए।"

Pumdikot Shiva Statue

The Pumdikot Shiva Statue, also known as the Pumdikot Mahadev Statue, is a prominent religious and tourist landmark located atop the Pumdikot hill near Pokhara, Nepal.

पुम्दिकोट शिवको मूर्ति, जसलाई पुम्दिकोट महादेवको मूर्तिको नामले पनि चिनिन्छ, यो नेपालको पोखरा नजिकै पुम्दिकोट पहाडको टुप्पोमा अवस्थित एक प्रमुख धार्मिक तथा पर्यटकीय स्थल हो।

Must-Try Food in Pokhara

Thakali Khana Set

A traditional Nepali meal originating from the Thak Khola region. It features steamed rice (bhat), lentil soup (dal), spicy fried spinach, potatoes, and a variety of tangy pickles (achar). It's a flavor explosion and a must-have lunch experience.

"थकाली खाना सेट मुस्ताङको थक खोला क्षेत्रबाट आएको एक परम्परागत नेपाली परिकार हो। यसमा भात, दाल, साग, आलु र विभिन्न प्रकारका अचारहरू समावेश हुन्छन्। यो पोखरा आउने हरेकले चाख्नै पर्ने स्वादिलो भोजन हो।"

Juicy Momos

No trip to Nepal is complete without Momos. These steamed dumplings filled with spiced minced meat or vegetables are served with a savory sesame and tomato dipping sauce (jhol achar). Perfect for any time of the day.

"नेपालको यात्रा मोमो बिना अपुरो हुन्छ। मसलेदार मासु वा तरकारीले भरिएको यी बाफमा पकाइएका डम्पलिङहरू तिल र गोलभेडाको झोल अचारसँग खाइन्छ। यो दिनको कुनै पनि समयमा खान सकिने लोकप्रिय खाजा हो।"

Best season to visit Pokhara

Autumn(Sep-Nov)

The most popular time to visit, as the monsoon rains have cleared the air, offering crystal-clear mountain views and optimal weather for trekking, paragliding, and cultural festivals like Dashain and Tihar. Temperatures are mild, ranging from approximately 10°C to 25°C.

Spring(March-May)

This is also an excellent season, known for mild temperatures (around 15°C to 30°C) and hillsides vibrant with blooming rhododendrons. The weather is favorable for outdoor activities, though there may be some haze in the afternoons closer to May.

Winter(Dec-Feb)

A quieter, off-season period with colder nights but crisp, clear days perfect for photography and short hikes at lower altitudes. Temperatures can drop to around 0°C to 5°C at night, but daytime temperatures are generally mild (10°C to 20°C).

Monsoon(June-August)

The off-season for most tourists due to heavy rainfall, which can cause travel disruptions. However, the landscape is incredibly lush and green, and it's the best time for budget travelers seeking fewer crowds and lower prices.

Fewa Lake

Fewa lake in Pokhara Nepal, is a scenic, second largest freshwater paraside.

Highlights

  • In the centre of the lake there is a famous temple called Tal Barahi Temple.

Travel Tips

Early morning or late afternoon is best. You can avoid crowds and take beautiful photos.

Best Season

The best time to visit Fewa Lake in Pokhara is during the autumn months of October to November, offering clear skies, pleasant temperatures ( ), and stunning Himalayan views. Spring (March to May) is another excellent, milder season with blooming rhododendrons, while the winter months (December to February) provide clear, sunny days, albeit colder.

Sarangkot Hill

Sarangkot is a popular 1,600m-high hilltop destination near Pokhara, Nepal, renowned for breathtaking sunrise and sunset views over the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges. Located just 5 km from Lakeside, it is a premier spot for paragliding, panoramic mountain views, and a short, scenic hiking destination.

Highlights

  • We can see beautiful scene of whole Pokhara from Sarangkot.

Travel Tips

Pack warm clothing for chilly mornings, bring cameras, and check weather forecasts for clear, mountain views.

Best Season

The best time to visit Sarangkot for the iconic sunrise over the Annapurna range is during the autumn (September to November) and spring (March to May) months, which offer clear, sunny, and stable weather conditions. Early morning (5:30 AM – 7:00 AM) is essential for the best views, with October being a peak month.

World Peace Pagoda

The World Peace Pagoda can be seen on top of a hill on the southern fringe of Phewa Lake. It has four images of the Buddha facing the four directions. The dome-shaped pagoda is an impressive sight, and its hilltop location commands a great view.

Highlights

  • Breathtaking panoramic views, serene meditation spaces, hiking trails, and nearby spiritual or natural attractions.

Travel Tips

Visit the World Peace Pagoda in Pokhara via a scenic boat ride across Fewa Lake followed by a 45-minute hike or take a 30-minute taxi ride to the top of Anadu Hill. Located at 1,100 meters, it offers stunning, panoramic views of the Annapurna range, Fishtail mountain, and the city.

Best Season

The best time to visit the World Peace Pagoda (Shanti Stupa) in Pokhara, Nepal, is during autumn (September to November) and spring (February to March). These seasons offer clear, sunny weather, providing the best visibility for panoramic mountain views, pleasant trekking conditions, and a vibrant atmosphere.

Pumdikot Shiva Statue

Discover the majestic Pumdikot Shiva Statue in Nepal, a calm spiritual monument offering panoramic vistas, lively culture, and the ideal place for nature lovers and devotees alike. Visitors at this very spot enjoy panoramic views of the Pokhara Valley, the Sarangkot hills, and the towering Annapurna Himalayan range-a destination that combines devotion with scenic splendor.

Highlights

  • The Pumdikot Shiva Statue is situated atop the serene hills of Pokhara and unites spirituality, art, and natural beauty in a dramatic fusion. This magnificent statue stands proud as the second tallest Shiva statue in Nepal, after the famous Kailashnath Mahadev.

Travel Tips

As this is a sacred religious site, dress modestly (shoulders and knees covered). You must remove your shoes to climb closer to the statue platform.Bring a light jacket for early mornings/evenings as it can get chilly. During monsoon (June–August), the trails can be very slippery and foggy.

Best Season

The best seasons to visit the Pumdikot Shiva Statue are Autumn (September to November) and Spring (March to May). These periods offer the clearest skies for panoramic views of the Annapurna mountain range and Phewa Lake, along with pleasant temperatures for the uphill trek.
